    Module Skeletons

    Skeleton presets and registry for creature body plans.

    Provides factory functions and pre-configured skeletons for common body types: biped, quadruped (small/medium/large), avian, and serpentine. Skeletons define bone hierarchies, joint constraints, and proportions used by the animation and creature composition systems.
